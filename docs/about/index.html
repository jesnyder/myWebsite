Jessica Snyder: Engineer, Inventor, Explorer

Hello. I use engineering principles to understand nature for the purpose of conservation. This has led me to work on a broad range of projects - from 3D printing human cells for tissue engineering to studing sea sponge for closed loop wastewater treatment aboard the International Space Station. My approach to research follows a consistent arc - use the power of observation to gain scientific understand and then apply that understanding for conservation. Many of those projects are presented on this website. Each endevour has given me humility, enthusium, and a deeper conviction that humanity already has many of the answer we seek.
